The Verdant Hearth

We believe that a farm is more than a place where food grows—it is a sanctuary of intention. At The Green Market Farm, our "Verdant Hearth" philosophy guides every seed we plant and every jar we seal.

It is a commitment to the slow process. To the richness of chemical-free soil, to the health of our local pollinators, and to the restorative power of a meal shared around a wooden table.
